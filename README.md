# ABC Live

ABC Live is an app that provides financial news. Its features are:

* Clickable news headlines leading to web pages of the news
* Updated news headlines

## Usage of the App

This app requires internet connection to show news headlines. Click on a headline to read more about that news.

## App Update

To update the app, quit the app (not close it but quit the app from overview or the click on the square or 3 lines button and quit) and open the app to update.
